Our History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world. Over the years, we've hosted thousands of attendees and hundreds of speakers from top tech companies and startups.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, promote collaboration, and empower individuals in the tech industry. We believe in diversity, accessibility, and continuous learning for all professionals—from beginners to experts.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Dr. Lovelace spoke at TechCon 2015 about the evolution of computing. Her insights continue to influence the next generation of developers.

Alan Turing

Alan Turing’s keynote in 2016 addressed the future of AI and ethical computing. His session remains one of the most viewed recordings in TechCon history.

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper shared stories of her programming journey and inspired thousands to pursue careers in software engineering at TechCon 2018.
